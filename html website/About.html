About

Education is the key to success

Our aim is to work together as educational professionals, with parents and other professional agencies so that together we can: motivate each child to discover their previously unlocked potential; encourage each child to discover their unique gifts and talents; and Work together as a team to help a child achieve both now and in the future. Vooruits vision is to provide a modenised school in the heart of Rundu. A school that offers an international curriculum in a small school setting. A Step Ahead is a public and selective combined school offering the National Curriculum from the Early Years Foundation Stage to Key Stage Five.

Admission:
  1. Benefit from the challenges and opportunities of the British National Curriculum;
  2. Benefit from a smaller school-setting like A Step Ahead
  3. Abide by the school’s rules and policies
  4. Contribute fully and positively to the A STEP AHEAD PRIMARY SCHOOL.
School Curriculumn:

    ​The learning and development of school-age children is heavily influenced by creativity and imaginative arts... Our curriculum at A STEP AHEAD PRIMARY SCHOOL in RUNDU, Namibia, is based on the English National Curriculum. Over 100 schools in the Namibia and abroad utilize this well accepted, highly valued curriculum. Its demonstrated efficacy and familiarity provide parents and pupils comfort—two qualities that are crucial when a family is relocating across borders.
