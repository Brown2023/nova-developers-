Welcome to the official website of A STEP AHEAD PRIMARY SCHOOL!

The Early Years Foundation Stage to Key Stage Three (Year 0 to Year 9) are currently covered by A STEP AHEAD PRIMARY SCHOOL, a coeducational, fully registered school.

We also teach the national languages of the following countries namely Angola,South Africa and Zambia.

​

We take great pride in providing the National Curriculum of Namibia, a strong, tried-and-true curriculum that fully prepares students for the General Certificate of Secondary Education (GCSE) and AS-Level exams.
